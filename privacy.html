Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The Cosmic Alchemy ("we", "us", Garima) respects your privacy. This page explains, in plain language, what information we collect and how it is used. This is a small, solo spiritual practice — we keep data handling minimal and intentional.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you choose to share when you email us or message us on Instagram (such as your name, email address, and the message you send).
  • Session details you provide for a booking (e.g. your intention, birth date/time for astrology or numerology). These are shared voluntarily by you.

This website is a static page. It does not run analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies, and it does not collect data in the background.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange your session.
  • To prepare and deliver the reading or healing you have booked.

Confidentiality

What you share within a session, or in preparation for one, is held with care and complete confidentiality. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Forms & email

The contact and booking buttons open your own email app with a pre-filled message; nothing is sent or stored until you choose to send it. Booking and payment details are arranged directly over email or Instagram.

Your choices
