About Me

✦

For a decade, I navigated the corporate architecture of a world that looked complete to the eye but felt silent to the soul. Amidst the height of external success, a singular whisper persisted: There is more.

That whisper became a summon, leading me away from the known and into the heart of the Divine. Through Reiki and celestial channels, my world underwent a profound transmutation — shifting from shadow into gold.

Today, I hold a sanctuary for those standing at their own sacred threshold. I do not provide the answers; I facilitate remembrance. I guide you inward, to the sovereign truth that has been waiting for you.
